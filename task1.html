Discover the Beauty of Tunisia

Experience rich culture, stunning landscapes, and unforgettable moments.

El Jem

El Jem is home to one of the world’s best-preserved Roman amphitheaters, where history comes alive in a breathtaking ancient setting.

Details

Stroll through the charming streets and discover local markets offering traditional crafts and delicious Tunisian flavors.

Experience the rich cultural heritage of El Jem through its museums and vibrant festivals that celebrate its unique past.

El Jem, Mahdia
80€ / Round Trip
El Kairouan

El Kairouan, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is considered the spiritual capital of Tunisia and boasts the Great Mosque— one of the oldest and most important in the Muslim world.

Details

Wander through the medina's narrow alleys filled with colorful carpets, handcrafted goods, and centuries of tradition.

Taste the city’s famous makroud (date-filled pastries) and immerse yourself in the unique blend of Islamic art, history, and Tunisian hospitality.

El Kairouan
120€ / Round Trip
Monastir

Monastir is a picturesque coastal city known for its golden beaches, vibrant marina, and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Details

Explore the impressive Ribat fortress and stroll through the old medina, where history and local life blend seamlessly.

Visit the grand mausoleum of Habib Bourguiba, Tunisia’s first president, and reflect on the city’s deep ties to the country’s modern history.

Monastir
75€ / Round Trip
Sidi Bou Said

Sidi Bou Said enchants visitors with its iconic white-and-blue architecture, perched on cliffs overlooking the sparkling Bay of Tunis.

Details

Stroll through cobblestone streets lined with art galleries, cafés, and jasmine-scented gardens that capture the town’s bohemian spirit.

Watch the sunset from a traditional café terrace while sipping mint tea and taking in one of the most romantic views in Tunisia.

Sidi Bou Said, Tunis
150€ / Round Trip
Sousse

Sousse blends ancient history with modern seaside charm, offering a lively atmosphere along its sandy beaches and bustling promenade.

Details

Step back in time as you explore the UNESCO-listed medina, filled with vibrant souks, historic mosques, and centuries-old ramparts.

Don’t miss the city's archaeological museum, home to one of the richest collections of Roman mosaics in North Africa.

Sousse
40€ / Round Trip
Djerba

Djerba is a sun-soaked island paradise known for its whitewashed villages, palm-fringed beaches, and serene Mediterranean vibes.

Details

Discover the island’s rich cultural tapestry by visiting El Ghriba, one of the oldest synagogues in the world, and exploring its diverse heritage.

Enjoy local markets, traditional Berber architecture, and the warm hospitality that makes Djerba a unique and unforgettable destination.

Djerba
110€ / Round Trip
